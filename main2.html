About Ayuni Travels

Your trusted partner for spiritual journeys

Founded in 2005, Ayuni Travels & Tours has been facilitating spiritual journeys to Makkah and Madina for thousands of pilgrims. With over 18 years of experience, we've perfected the art of creating meaningful, comfortable, and spiritually fulfilling Hajj and Umrah experiences.

Our team of Islamic scholars, travel experts, and logistics professionals work together to ensure every aspect of your journey is handled with care, respect, and attention to detail. We understand the significance of this spiritual journey and strive to make it as seamless as possible.
