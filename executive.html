5 Star Umrah Packages

Our 5 Star Umrah package provides accommodation in 4-star and 5-star hotels in Makkah and Madinah. Enjoy breakfast, room service, Wi-Fi, and air-conditioned rooms. Hotels are located very close to Haramain Shareefain, ensuring easy access to Masjid Al-Haram and Masjid Nabawi.

Five Star Hotels in Makkah

In Makkah, you will stay at premium hotels such as Hilton Suites Makkah, Swissotel Makkah, Fairmont Clock Royal Tower, Hilton Tower, Makkah Tower, Pullman Zamzam Makkah, Movenpick Hotel, Makkah Millennium Hotel, and Dar Al Tawhid (Intercontinental Hotel Makkah). These hotels are at zero distance from Masjid Al-Haram.

Five Star Hotels in Madinah

In Madinah, options include Pullman Zamzam Hotel, Madinah Marriott Hotel, Millennium Al Aqeeq Hotel, Crown Plaza, Dar Al Taqwa Hotel, Movenpick Hotel, Madinah Hilton, Shaza Al Madinah, Al Eiman Royal Hotel, Al Haram, and Rove Hotel. We also provide any hotel as per your preference, all located close to Masjid Al-Haram and Masjid Nabawi.

Umrah Transport

Ayuni Travel provides luxury cars for pick-up and drop-off, included in our Executive Umrah Package. Travel in VIP vehicles such as Toyota, Land Cruiser, GMC, or private cars and Hiace, tailored to your family size and preferences, available at Jeddah and Madinah airports.

Umrah Visa Service

Our 5 Star Umrah package includes Umrah Visa, E-Visa, sticker visa, and tourist visa services. Fulfill the visa requirements, book your package online, and receive your Umrah visa at your doorstep.

Umrah Flights

Ayuni Travel offers competitive rates on Umrah return tickets. Book your flights online at affordable prices, with direct flights to Makkah and Madinah via Dubai. We also offer combined Umrah and Turkey tour packages.

Umrah Ziyarat

Our Executive Umrah package includes Ziyarat to the holy places of Makkah and Madinah, making your trip memorable and spiritually enriching.

Ziyarat in Makkah

Visit Masjid Al Haram, Masjid Aisha (Second largest Mosque in Makkah), Mount Arafat (Jabal Rehmat), Jannatul Maula, Jabal an-Nour, Jabal Thawr (Ghar-e-Thor), Ghar-e-Hira, Masjid-e-Nimra, Birth Place of the Holy Prophet (PBUH), National Library of Makkah, Masjid Jinn, Mount Abu Qubays, and Al Khaif Mosque.

Ziyarat in Madinah

Visit Masjid-e-Quba, Masjid-e-Nabawi, Riyaad-ul-Jannah, Masjid Abu Bakar, Masjid-e-Jumah, Masjid Bilal, Masjid-e-Khandaq, Masjid Miqat Bir Ali, Jannat-Ul-Baqi, Masjid-e-Qiblatain, and Wadi E Al-Baida (Wadi Jinn).

FAQs

Which things are covered in the Executive Umrah Package?

The Executive Umrah Package includes Umrah visa, accommodation, and luxury transport. Ziyarat to holy places in Makkah and Madinah is also included. Stay in 4-star and 5-star hotels and travel in air-conditioned VIP cars for a comfortable experience.

What is the hotel distance in the Executive Umrah Package?

Accommodations are in 4-star and 5-star hotels, located very close to Masjid Al-Haram and Masjid Nabawi, some at zero meters. Options in Makkah include Anjum Hotel, Hilton Suites, and Swissotel; in Madinah, options include Pullman Zamzam, Madinah Marriott, and Dar Al Taqwa. You can suggest preferred hotels.

What’s the duration of the Executive Umrah Package?

Durations vary, offering 7, 10, or 15 days, extendable up to 30 days. The package includes luxury facilities from hotel bookings to transportation.

How to book an Umrah Visa online?

Book your Umrah E-Visa online from anywhere (e.g., UK, Canada, Turkey, Dubai, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Sri Lanka). Send a scanned passport and photo, and receive your Umrah E-Visa within 24 hours.

Is the Executive Umrah Package very expensive?

Executive Umrah Packages are reasonably priced, offering luxury services including 4-star and 5-star accommodations and VIP transport, ensuring comfort without excessive costs, depending on the package selected.
