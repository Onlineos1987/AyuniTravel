Frequently Asked Questions

Can we customize the Umrah Package?

Yes, Ayuni Travel provides the facility to customize your Umrah package. You can choose hotels, modify breakfast menus, select transport options, and tailor other requirements to your needs.

Can we visit Riyadh on an Umrah Visa?

Yes, Umrah visa holders can visit any city in Saudi Arabia, not just Makkah and Madinah, allowing pilgrims to explore other regions.

What is the validity of the Umrah Visa?

The Umrah Visa is valid for 30 days per the Hijri Calendar, starting from the flight date. It is for performing Umrah and visiting holy sites, not for work or residency.

How do I book hotels for Umrah?

Consult Ayuni Travel to book hotels close to Masjid al-Haram and Masjid Nabawi. Ensure the hotel's distance suits your needs to avoid long walks.

When does the Umrah season start after Hajj?

The Umrah season starts two months after Hajj. Contact Ayuni Travel early to secure your Umrah Visa and package.
