15 Days Umrah Package 2024-2025

The Umrah pilgrimage is a profound spiritual experience that cleanses the soul and strengthens one's faith. At Ayuni Travel, we understand how important this sacred journey is.

We are dedicated to providing you with a smooth and enriching Umrah experience. Our 15-day Umrah package from Pakistan is carefully planned to ensure your pilgrimage is comfortable, spiritually fulfilling, and memorable.

Comprehensive 15 Days Umrah Package

Our 15-day Umrah Package offers a comprehensive itinerary that covers all essential aspects of the pilgrimage, from travel arrangements to accommodation and guided tours. We aim to provide a hassle-free experience so you can focus entirely on your spiritual journey.

Package Inclusions:

  • Round-trip Airfare: Flights from major cities in Pakistan to Jeddah or Madinah, ensuring a convenient and comfortable journey.
  • Visa Processing: Our team handles all visa requirements and paperwork, ensuring a smooth and stress-free process.
  • Luxurious Accommodation: Stay in 4-star or 5-star hotels near Haram in Makkah and Madinah, offering luxury and convenience.
  • Transportation: Private air-conditioned transport for airport transfers and travel between Makkah and Madinah.
  • Guided Tours: Experienced guides offer insights and information about rituals and historical significance of the sites.
  • Meals: Daily breakfast and dinner, with several options to enhance your trip.
  • Ziarat Tours: Visits to significant religious and historical sites in Makkah and Madinah.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Departure from Pakistan

  • Depart from your city in Pakistan and arrive in Jeddah or Madinah.
  • Meet and assist at the airport, followed by transfer to your hotel in Makkah.
  • Check in and rest after your journey.

Day 2-4: Perform Umrah in Makkah

  • Guided assistance for performing Umrah rituals: Tawaf, Sa'i, and Halq/Taqsir.
  • Spend your days in the holy city of Makkah, engaging in prayers and exploring the Haram.
  • Visit important sites like Jabal al-Noor, Jabal Thawr, and Masjid Aisha.

Day 5-8: Explore Makkah

  • Participate in guided Ziarat tours to significant landmarks such as Mina, Muzdalifah, Arafat, and the Cave of Hira.
  • Free time for individual worship and personal reflection.

Day 9: Travel to Madinah

  • Check-out from your hotel in Makkah and travel to Madinah via private air-conditioned transport.
  • Check in at your hotel in Madinah and rest.

Day 10-13: Discover the Holy City of Madinah

  • Visit the Prophet's Mosque (Masjid an-Nabawi) and offer prayers.
  • Participate in guided Ziarat tours to significant sites like Quba Mosque, Qiblatain Mosque, and Jannat al-Baqi.
  • Spend your days in spiritual activities and exploring the historical sites of Madinah.

Day 14: Return Preparation

  • Enjoy your last day in Madinah, performing prayers and shopping for souvenirs.
  • Check out from the hotel and prepare for your return journey.

Day 15: Return to Pakistan

  • Transfer to the airport for your return flight to Pakistan.
  • Arrive back home with cherished memories and a spiritually enriched heart.
